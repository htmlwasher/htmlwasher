Re: What happened with Arch Linux ISO in 2024

archlinux-2024.05.01-x86_64.iso changed the UEFI boot loader from GRUB back to systemd-boot (which was used in archlinux-2022.06.01-x86_64.iso and prior ISOs) which means the kernel and initramfs need to be duplicated; see https://gitlab.archlinux.org/archlinux/ … quests/374 for details. The rest of the size increase is just general bloat accumulation. I.e. the kernel and linux-firmware get bigger with each release.
