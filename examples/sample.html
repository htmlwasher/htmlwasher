How boilerplate removal works

By Jane Doe · 2026-07-09

Boilerplate removal keeps a page's main content and discards the navigation, sidebars, advertisements, and footers that surround it. Trafilatura scores candidate nodes and keeps the subtree that most plausibly carries the article body.

Why page type matters

An article, a forum thread, and a product listing each hide their main content in a different shape. trafilaturacore classifies the page first, then routes extraction through a profile tuned for that type.
